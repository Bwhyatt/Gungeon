How to play the game:
Press play in the main.py script. keep trying to play the game over and over until it allows you

Move around using WASD

You will be in either sword or gun mode all the time. To switch between these modes, press "V"

If you are in Gun mode you can left click to shoot and press "R" to reload
If you are in sword mode you can left click to slash your sword and right click to parry. Parrying will work on an enemy hitting you with a melee attack.
A parry will stun the enemy and depending on your sword may give you some buff.

Your goal is to kill all the enemies inside the dungeon and not die from their attacks.
